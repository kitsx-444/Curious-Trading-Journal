# Curious-Trading-Journal

A simple trade journal for forex traders to log their trades and instantly see their performance summary.

This program requires user input such as the direction of the trade, the entry price, exit price and pair name. It calculates the profitability of your trade, scores it and gives you a complete summary at the end 
of the program.

I learned about using dictionaries, and enumeration. I also dove deeper into for loops. Starting only 10 days ago I am proud of the progress I've made - not watching endless tutorials but learning through doing - despite not having a full grasp on them but with repetition I trust I will get there.
